After professing his love to Sarah and sharing a sensual goodnight kiss, Evan is filled with joy. Although Sarah doesn't voice her love, Evan sees it in her eyes and feels it in her kiss. Entering his luxurious hotel room for the first time, he's thrilled by the opulence, feeling that everything in his world is exactly as it should be. As he settles in, an envelope slipped under his door catches his eye. Inside, he finds a key card to Sarah's hotel room and a handwritten note saying "I'm ready," leaving him with a sense of anticipation and excitement.
