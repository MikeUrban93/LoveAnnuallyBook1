Oblivious to Sarah's behind-the-scenes efforts and personal financial contributions to feeding the volunteers, Evan's friends criticize her for not actively participating in the debris cleanup. Their frustration grows when Evan chooses to skip the next day's softball game, furthering their discontent with Sarah's influence on him. A few of Evan's friends, reaching a breaking point, give Patti an ultimatum: either she warns Evan about Sarah, or Roxanne will do so. Feeling cornered, Patti feels she must comply.
